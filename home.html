Frequently Asked Questions

What is Talent Match?
Talent Match is a platform that connects users based on their skills and expertise. Users can post queries and get matched with others who have the required skills to collaborate on tasks or projects.
How do I register?
To register, simply click on the 'Register' button on the homepage and provide your personal and professional details, including your skills and work experience.
How does the system match my query with users?
The system uses a query-to-user matching algorithm that analyzes the skills and experience specified in your query and compares them with the registered users' profiles.
Can I post a query without creating an account?
No, you must be a registered user to post a query. Registration ensures that your profile is complete and accurate.
What types of queries can I post?
You can post a variety of queries, including hiring requests, requests for expert advice or mentorship, collaboration opportunities for research, and help with tasks or problem-solving.
How do I connect with other users?
Once your query is matched with relevant users, you can contact them using external communication tools like Google Meet, Zoom, or other preferred methods. The platform does not have an in-built messaging system.
Can I post a query without creating an account?
No, you must be a registered user to post a query. Registration ensures that your profile is complete and accurate.
Is there a fee to use Talent Match?
Talent Match is free for all users. You can register, post queries, and connect with other users without any charges. Premium features may be introduced in the future.
Can I update my profile after registration?
Yes, you can update your profile at any time. Simply log into your account and go to the profile settings to modify your personal information, skills, or work experience.
How can I be sure that the users I connect with are reliable?
Talent Match encourages profile transparency. Users are encouraged to upload resumes, portfolios, or recommendations. You can also review user profiles and initiate a video call before engaging in collaboration.
Can I use Talent Match for personal or academic purposes?
Yes, Talent Match is not limited to professional networking. You can use it for academic purposes or personal projects, whether you're seeking guidance, help, or collaboration.
What happens if the user I connect with is not responsive?
If a user is not responsive, you can reach out to other users who may be available. The platform facilitates connections, but responsiveness is ultimately up to the users.
Can I collaborate with multiple people at once?
Yes, you can collaborate with multiple users at the same time. Talent Match supports collaboration on different queries or projects simultaneously.
How do I post a query?
To post a query, simply log in to your account and navigate to the "Post Query" section. Enter the details of your request, including the skills or expertise you're seeking, and submit.
Is my data secure on Talent Match?
Yes, we ensure that your data is stored securely and follows industry-standard security practices to protect your personal information and privacy.
